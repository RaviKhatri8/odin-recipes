Mango Lassi

Mango lassi is a very rich and luxurious drink with yogurt and mangoes.

It is the perfect summer drink when served chilled.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups mangos - peeled, seeded, chopped, and chilled.
  • 1 ½ cups plain yogurt.
  • 1 ½ cold milk.
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream.
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar.
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom.

Directions

  1. Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, confectioners' sugar, and cardamom in a blender.

  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.

  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
